## About me  
- I am a lawyer graduated from the Universidad Nacional del Litoral, in the exercise of the profession.  
  

- Since I was little, I have been interested in the world of computing and programming, so I decided to start studying web development, with the aim of being able to practice the two professions (legal & tech) that generate so much enthusiasm in me.
  
  

- I am finishing the Fullstack Web Developer course that is taught through the Henry platform, where I was able to gain knowledge and experience working on projects based on JavaScript and related technologies such as React, Redux, NodeJs, Express, Sequalize, SQL; frameworks and libraries like MaterialUI, Auth0, among others, and of course HTML and CSS too.  
  

- I consider that I have many technical skills and great potential in the IT world. I am organized, dedicated and responsible with the objectives.  
  

- I easily adapt to new challenges and my greatest ability is to be able to acquire new skills and knowledge very quickly, which would imply a highly valuable addition to any work team aimed at achieving excellent results according to the needs of each project or customer requirement.
